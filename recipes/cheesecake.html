Ingredients

  • 1 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped pecans
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 cups apples - peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup chopped pecans

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In a large bowl, stir together the graham cracker crumbs, 1/2 cup finely chopped pecans, 3 tablespoons sugar, 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon, and melted butter.
  3. Press this mixture into the bottom of a 9 inch springform pan.
  4. Bake the crust for 10 minutes.
  5. In a large bowl, combine cream cheese and 1/2 cup sugar. Mix at medium speed until smooth. Beat in eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Blend in vanilla; pour to fill the baked crust.
  6. In a small bowl, stir together 1/3 cup sugar and 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon. Toss the cinnamon sugar with the apples to cost.
  7. Spoon mixture over cream cheese layer and sprinkt with 1/4 chopped pecans.
  8. Bake in oven for 60 to 70 minutes. With a knife loosen cake from rim of pan. Let cool, then remove the rim of pan. Chill cake before serving.
  9. Enjoy!
