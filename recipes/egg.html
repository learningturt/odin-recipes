Soft Hard-Boiled Eggs

HOME

This is the best quick and easy snack!

This is the perfect snack when you need somthing quick and easy. It's quick to cook and quick to eat! It also does not require a lot of equipment which is perfect for someone lazy like me.

This is also perfect for someone that is trying to keep up their protein goals.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups water
  • 6 large cold eggs

Steps

  1. Pour the water into a medium saucepan with a lid.
  2. Place over high heat and bring it to a low boil.
  3. Place the eggs in the water. Then cover the pan and reduce the heat to medium-high. Cook for 9 1/2 minutes.
  4. Remove pan from heat and cool eggs down with cold running water, tipping out the water, and continuing to run cold water over the eggs until they are cool.
  5. Enjoy!
