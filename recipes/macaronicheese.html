The Best Macaroni and Cheese Recipe

HOME

This is a sponsored recipe.

I was recently contacted by Borden and they challenged me to make the best macaroni and cheese that I could using their preshredded cheeses. To be quite honest, I am not a fan of preshredded cheeses. I find that they don't melt well and the oil tends to separate easily. However, recently I've been through a rut and I wanted to try something new to revitalize my cooking mood.

I found that using a lot of cheese and cream was the answer to my shredded cheese problems(all from Borden of course). I was able to perfect my recipe to the point that my youngest child (also the pickiest) was able to eat 3 servings! The recipe I have created is written below.

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups of Borden Tripcle Cheddar Natural Shreds
  • 1 cup Borden Colby Jack Natural Shreds
  • 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 2 tablesoons Borden bButter
  • 2 tablespoons all purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup Borden Vitamin D Milk
  • 1 cup Borden Half&Half
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Spray the inside of a 1 1/2-quart casserole dish with non-stick cooking spray. Combine Cheddar and Colby jack cheeses in a medium bowl and set aside.
  3. Cook macaroni in boiling salted water according to package directions; drain well and set aside.
  4. While macaroni is cooking, melt butter in a medium saucepan over low heat. Blend in flour, stirring constantly, until smooth and bubbly.
  5. Gradually whisk in milk and half&half. Stir constantly, until mixture boils and thickens (about 2 to 3 minutes).
  6. Remove from heat. Add cayenne and black pepper, and 2 cups of the reserved cheese mixture; stir until cheese is melted and sauce is smooth.
  7. Stir cooked macaroni into the sauce and season to taste. Place in the prepared casserole dish. Top with remaining 1/2 cup of cheese and dot with additional butter, if desired.
  8. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes or until lightly browned.
  9. Enjoy!
